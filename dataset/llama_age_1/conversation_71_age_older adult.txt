ASSISTANT: Absolutely! Here's the list of ingredients you'll need:

* 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
* 1/2 cup granulated sugar
* 1/4 cup melted butter
* 1 1/2 cups chocolate chip cookie dough
* 1 cup mini marshmallows
* 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

And here are the instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a medium bowl, mix together the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until well combined.
3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
5. Allow the crust to cool completely.
6. Cut the cookie dough into small pieces and spread them evenly over the cooled crust.
7. Place the marshmallows on top of the cookie dough.
8. Bake for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the marshmallows are golden brown and the edges are set.
9. Allow the treats to cool completely before cutting them into squares and serving.
